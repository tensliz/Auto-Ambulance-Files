About Us

History of Auto Ambulance

With over three decades of experience in the automotive repair business, the owner and operator of this establishment, Fernando, began his journey as a lube tech at a gas station. His dedication led him to pursue formal education at St. Phillips College, where he earned his ASE certification, solidifying his expertise in the field. At the age of 23, fueled by a robust work ethic and a passion for cars, Fernando took the entrepreneurial leap and established his own automotive shop. Known for his tireless work and commitment, Fernando treats every car that comes through the shop as if it were his own, ensuring a level of care and attention that reflects his extensive experience and enjoyment for the craft.
